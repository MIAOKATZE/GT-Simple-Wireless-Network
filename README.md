A GregTech New Horizons mod that adds **wireless energy monitoring, transfer, and redstone control** to the GTNH wireless EU network — all craftable at LV tier.

一个 GregTech New Horizons 模组，为 GTNH 无线 EU 网络添加**无线能量监控、传输和红石控制**——全部可在 LV 阶段合成。

> [!NOTE]
> This is an unofficial mod. Please avoid discussing it in official GTNH forums.
> 这是一个非官方模组，讨论此模组时请注意场合。

## 无线能量监视器 / Wireless Energy Monitor

<p align="center">
<img src="images/Wireless_Energy_Monitor_CN.png" width="500"><br>
<em>无线能量监视器 / Wireless Energy Monitor</em>
</p>

实时显示无线电网能量状态（每 5 秒采样），支持 5 种红石模式与参数化阈值，可连接工业信息屏。

Real-time wireless grid energy status (5s sampling) with 5 redstone modes, parametric thresholds, and Industrial Info Panel integration.

<img src="images/Wireless_Energy_Monitor_UI_CN.png" width="400"><br>
<em>监视器 GUI / Monitor GUI</em>

## 便携无线监测终端 / Portable Monitor

<p align="center">
<img src="images/README-Portable_Wireless_Network_Monitor-CN1.png" width="400">
<img src="images/README-Portable_Wireless_Network_Monitor-CN2.png" width="400"><br>
<em>充电状态（左）& 放电状态（右）/ Charging (left) & Discharging (right)</em>
</p>

手持设备，放入背包（含 Baubles 饰品栏）即自动显示 HUD，实时监控电网能量与 EU/t。

Handheld device; place in inventory (incl. Baubles slots) for auto HUD display with real-time grid energy and EU/t.

---

## 网络信息屏 / Network Info Panel

<p align="center">
<img src="images/Network Info Panel_CN.png" width="600"><br>
<em>网络信息屏 / Network Info Panel</em>
</p>

多方块显示面板，主屏 + 拓展屏可拼接成任意矩形尺寸，支持 4 时间窗口（5m/1h/8h/24h）。

Multi-block display panel; main + extender panels form any rectangular size, supports 4 time windows (5m/1h/8h/24h).

### EU 走势图 / EU Chart

<p align="center">
<img src="images/Network Info Panel_L_CN.png" width="600"><br>
<em>EU 走势图 — 全天候检测 / EU Chart — All-Weather Monitoring</em>
</p>

无线电网能量趋势图，双 Y 轴显示能量与 EU/t，Fritsch-Carlson 单调三次 Hermite 样条平滑渲染。

Wireless grid energy trend chart with dual Y-axis (energy + EU/t), rendered with Fritsch-Carlson monotone cubic Hermite spline.

### AE 走势图 / AE Chart

<p align="center">
<img src="images/Network Info Panel_AE1.png" width="500"><br>
<em>AE 走势图 / AE Chart</em>
</p>

绑定 AE 物品/流体，双 Y 轴显示存量与变化率趋势，支持 4 时间窗口。

Bind AE items/fluids; dual Y-axis display of stock and change rate trends across 4 time windows.

### AE 实时监测 / AE Realtime Monitor

<p align="center">
<img src="images/Network Info Panel_AE2.png" width="500"><br>
<em>AE 实时监测 / AE Realtime Monitor</em>
</p>

同时监控多个 AE 物品/流体，可滚动列表显示存量、实时变化率与 300s 均值。

Monitor multiple AE items/fluids simultaneously; scrollable list with stock, realtime rate, and 300s average.

## 无线网络链路终端 / Wireless Energy Tap

<p align="center">
<img src="images/README-Portable_Wireless_Network_Tap.jpg" width="400"><br>
<em>链路终端与覆盖板 / Energy Tap and Covers</em>
</p>

便携物品，右键机器安装覆盖板，将任意机器接入无线电网。支持能源模式（从电网取电）与动力模式（向电网送电）。

Portable item; right-click machines to install covers and connect them to the wireless grid. Supports Energy mode (draw from grid) and Power mode (output to grid).
